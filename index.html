tutorial about the game

        

            
    
                
    • 
                
    • 
                
    • 
                
    • 
            

            
The game can move on the WASD keys and place bombs on the arrows in 4 directions

            
unlike the player , the enemies perform an action every 2 turns . On his turn, the enemy can descend vertically or diagonally, or attack the player if he is in close proximity. If they see a player, they will go to the point where they saw him last.

            

                The player has 4 types of bombs and one available action in the shop.

                

                Default Bomb: A basic bomb available from the start, it explodes in a cross pattern. All other bombs cost money.

                

                Detonation: Costs 1 coin and forces the bomb to explode immediately upon activation.

                

                Square Bomb: Costs 2 coins and has a 3x3 explosion radius.

                

                Random Bomb: Costs 3 coins and creates a random shape as its explosion radius.

                

                Super Bomb: Costs 4 coins and damages all cells diagonally and vertically from the explosion point.
            

            
            

                The game has no ending; the goal is to progress through as many randomly generated dungeon floors as possible in one run.
                

                To complete the current dungeon floor, you must eliminate all enemies (E) on the map and enter the portal (@), which teleports you to the next floor.
                

                For an easier playthrough, it’s recommended to collect coins (c) and use them to buy bombs or detonate bombs in the shop.
